Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 4, 2026

Sharma Education IGNOU ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data, system permissions, advertisements, transactions, and user inquiries when you use our mobile application.

1. Information Collection and Usage

To provide core functionalities like user authentication, issue resolution, premium transactions, and cloud syncing, the app interacts securely with specific services and device capabilities:

A. Authentication Data

Google Sign-In & Firebase Auth: To access the app's features, you are required to log in securely using Google Sign-In via Firebase Authentication. This service verifies your identity safely. We do not store your personal credentials or passwords on our own external servers.

B. Customer Support & Inquiry Data

When you use the "Contact Us" feature within the app to report an issue or submit feedback, we collect specific information you voluntarily provide, including your **email address, issue title, and issue description**. This data is securely sent to and stored in our **Firebase Firestore** cloud database. We use this information exclusively to communicate with you, investigate technical problems, and resolve your issues.

C. In-App Purchases & Premium Features

The app offers premium educational materials, including premium notes, assignments, and solved papers through In-App Purchases. All transactions are securely processed directly via the Google Play Billing system. We do not collect, view, or store your credit/debit card numbers, bank details, or payment credentials. Google Play handles your financial transactions securely and only provides us with transaction tokens to unlock your purchased content.

D. Cloud Services & Data Storage

  • Firebase Firestore & Firebase Storage: The app utilizes Firebase Firestore to manage application metadata, track purchase status, and store user-submitted support inquiries. Firebase Storage is used to securely host educational resources.
  • Local Storage & Room Database: Downloaded PDF files are stored directly in your device’s secure internal storage. We use a local Room Database solely to track, organize, and manage your downloaded PDFs locally on your device.

2. Device Permissions Required

To ensure reliable data synchronization, file downloading, and secure transaction flows, the app requests the following system permissions:

  • Internet Access (INTERNET): Required to connect to Firebase cloud services, submit support requests to Firestore, process secure transactions via Google Play Billing, serve advertisements, and download educational PDF files.
  • Network State (ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE): Used to monitor device connectivity. This ensures the app only attempts content downloads or data syncs when an active internet connection is available, preserving your battery and mobile data.
  • Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS): Required (on Android 13 and above) to display notifications that keep you informed about active PDF downloads and background data synchronization status.
  • Foreground Service (FOREGROUND_SERVICE & FOREGROUND_SERVICE_DATA_SYNC): Used to run critical data synchronization and file downloading tasks in the background. This ensures that your educational PDF downloads complete successfully even if you navigate away from the app.

3. Advertisements (Google AdMob)

To keep our basic educational resources accessible, we display advertisements via Google AdMob. These ads may appear when you attempt to download files within the application.

AdMob may use and collect anonymous tracking data, such as your device's Advertising ID (e.g., Android Advertising ID or AAID), to serve personalized or contextual advertisements, prevent fraud, and analyze ad performance. You can manage or reset your advertising identifiers at any time through your Android device's built-in Google Settings menu.

4. Third-Party Services

The app utilizes trusted third-party Software Development Kits (SDKs) to handle authentication, advertising, cloud storage, and transactions. These services may collect information used to identify you in accordance with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google Play Billing System (In-App Purchases)
  • Google AdMob
  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Firestore & Cloud Storage

Note: We highly recommend reviewing Google’s Privacy Policy to understand how they handle data processed through their services: Google Privacy & Terms.

5. Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data. By leveraging Google Play’s secure billing framework, storing user support inquiries and metadata securely within Firebase’s cloud infrastructure, and keeping your download history local within a Room Database on your device, we minimize the risk of unauthorized data exposure. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our application provides educational resources. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that their child has provided us with personal information (such as through our support form) without their consent, they should contact us immediately so we can take necessary actions to delete the records.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect functional updates or regulatory changes. Any modifications will be reflected on this page with an updated "Effective Date." You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates.
